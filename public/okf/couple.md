# Captain Bond Couple

A private space for couples with daily question decks, a harmony gauge, and deep connection games. Strengthen your bond, one question at a time.

## Features

- **Daily question decks** — fresh questions generated each session (fun, deep, intimate)
- **Harmony gauge** — both partners rate the conversation; tracks connection trend over time
- **Private** — answers visible only to you and your partner. No data shared.
