# Captain Bond Party

Turn your TV into a party game board. 5 game modes — Icebreaker, Spicy, Deep Connection, Imposteur, Date Night. 3 free cards to try, then unlock unlimited play.

## How it works

1. Host opens captainbond.com on the TV screen
2. Players scan a QR code with their phone
3. The game board displays on TV, players answer on their phones
4. Scores and results reveal on the big screen

## Game modes

| Mode | Vibe | Players |
|------|------|---------|
| Icebreaker | Light, fun, get-to-know-you | 2-50+ |
| Spicy | Bold, flirty, daring | 2-50+ |
| Deep Connection | Meaningful conversations | 2-20 |
| Imposteur | Deception & deduction (social deduction) | 4-20 |
| Date Night | Romantic, intimate | 2 |

## Pricing

- 3 free cards per mode

- Monthly subscription: 9.99€
