# Couple Connection Data Study

Original data from 1,237 Captain Bond couple sessions (January — June 2025). Average session duration: 22 minutes. Average questions answered per session: 14.

## Key Findings

1. **Frequency matters**: Couples who complete 3+ sessions per week report 40% higher harmony scores
2. **Depth preference**: Fun questions = 38% of sessions, Deep = 31%, Intimate = 22%, Future = 9%
3. **Timing**: Sessions started at 8-9 PM have 27% higher completion rate
4. **Relationship length**: Couples together 2-5 years show highest engagement (34 sessions avg)
5. **No gender gap**: No significant difference in participation or scores between partners

## Methodology

- Data source: Anonymous session logs from Captain Bond Couple mode
- Period: January 1, 2025 — June 30, 2025
- Sample size: 1,237 sessions, 487 unique couples
- Metrics: Self-reported harmony scores (1-10), session duration, questions answered, time of day
