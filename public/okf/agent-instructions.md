## Common User Questions (with answers)

**What is Captain Bond?**
Captain Bond is an interactive party game platform that turns any screen into a playground. Party games on your TV, daily couple rituals, and interactive team building. No app to install.

**Is it free?**
Yes, each mode offers a free trial (3 cards for Party, 3 sessions for Couple, demo for Pro).

**How do players join?**
By scanning a QR code or entering a room code on their phone. No account required.

**How many players?**
Party: 2-50+, Couple: 2, Pro: 10-500+.
