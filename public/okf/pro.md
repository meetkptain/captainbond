# Captain Bond Pro

Interactive entertainment solution for bars, cafés, corporate team building, and employee onboarding. Giant-screen games with AI voice DJ — zero logistics.

## Use cases

- **Bars & cafés** — 99€/month subscription, fills weeknight tables, AI voice DJ hosts automatically
- **Corporate team building** — 299€ HT per session, 10-500+ participants, in-person or hybrid
- **Employee onboarding** — Icebreaker games for new hires, culture integration
- **HR / QVT** — Social climate surveys disguised as games

## Key benefits

- 22% average increase in beverage orders for bars
- No staff needed to host — AI voice DJ handles everything
- Players join from their phone — no app, no account
