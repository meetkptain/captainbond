# Captain Bond Platform

Captain Bond is an interactive party game platform that turns any screen into a playground. Three modes serve three distinct audiences:

- **Party** — 5 game modes on your TV (Icebreaker, Spicy, Deep Connection, Imposteur, Date Night). 2-50+ players. 3 free cards to try.
- **Couple** — Private space with daily question decks, harmony gauge, and deep connection games. One question at a time.
- **Pro** — Interactive entertainment for bars, cafés, and corporate events. AI voice DJ, 500+ participants, zero logistics.

## Key differentiators

- No app download required — players join via phone browser with a QR code or room code
- Works on any screen with a browser: smart TV, Chromecast, Apple TV, laptop via HDMI
- Bilingual (EN + FR)

## Core metrics

- 50,000+ questions generated
- 1,200+ couple sessions
- 10,000+ players hosted
