# Couple Questions Knowledge Base

Source: Captain Bond Couple Connection Data Study (1,237 sessions) and captainbond.com/blog

## Relationship Stages & Question Types

### New Relationship (0-6 months)
Focus: Fun, light, getting-to-know
Best time: During dates or walks
Questions: What song makes you dance? What fictional character would you want as roommate? Pineapple on pizza? If you could have any superpower? What was your childhood fear? What's your guilty pleasure TV show? Beach or mountains? What app do you use too much? What's your go-to karaoke song?

### Established Couple (6 months-3 years)
Focus: Deeper connection, values, childhood, dreams
Best time: Quiet evenings at home
Questions: What did you want to be at ten? Who was your first role model? What family tradition do you want to keep? What memory always makes you smile? What's the best advice you received? What book changed your perspective? What does a perfect Sunday look like? What's your love language? What makes you feel safe enough to be honest?

### Long-Term Partner (3-10 years)
Focus: Maintaining intimacy, growth, change over time
Best time: Weekend mornings, dedicated couple time
Questions: How have you changed since we met? What do you need from me that you're not getting? What's a dream you haven't told me about? What do you miss about our early days? How can we keep growing together?

### Engaged/Married
Focus: Building future, family, legacy, money
Best time: During planning sessions, quiet evenings
Questions: Where do you see us in 5 years? What kind of home feels right? How do you feel about money and freedom? What legacy do you want to leave? How do you want to handle conflict as a team?

### Rediscovering
Focus: Rekindling, new chapters, reinvention
Best time: During a dedicated date night
Questions: What would you want to try again together? How have we changed? What's something new you'd like to explore? What do you still find surprising about me?

## Key Data Points (from 1,237 sessions)
- Couples with 3+ sessions/week report 40% higher harmony scores
- Question preference: Fun 38%, Deep 31%, Intimate 22%, Future 9%
- Best time for conversations: 8-9 PM (27% higher completion)
- Peak engagement: couples together 2-5 years
- No significant difference between partners in participation

## Tips for Great Conversations
1. Pick a weekly slot — 20 minutes is enough
2. One person asks, the other answers — then swap
3. Either partner can pass any question without explaining
4. Follow the energy — if an answer opens a door, walk through it
5. Put phones away
6. Create a safety zone — no judgment, no problem-solving
