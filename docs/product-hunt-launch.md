## Maker Comment (first comment)

Hi Product Hunt! 👋

We built Captain Bond because we believe the best conversations and connections don't happen by accident — they need the right environment and the right prompts.

Captain Bond turns any TV into a party game board, gives couples daily question decks with a harmony gauge, and helps bars/corporate teams engage groups of any size. No app download needed — just a QR code.

What started as a simple "questions for couples" idea grew into a platform serving 3 audiences:
- 🎉 Party hosts (5 game modes, 2-50+ players)
- 💕 Couples (daily rituals, harmony gauge, deep connection games)
- 🏢 Bars & companies (AI voice DJ, 500+ participants, zero logistics)

We're live at captainbond.com — would love your feedback!
